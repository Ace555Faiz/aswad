My Education

I have been through studies in four places starting from kindergarten, primary school, secondary school and university. When I was 5 to 6 years old, I studied in a Tadika Kemas in Kampung Jabi. That is my father's village. When I was 7 to 9 years old, I studied at Sekolah Kebangsaan Tualang. When I was 10 to 12 years old, I studied at Sekolah Kebangsaan Pulau Nyior. When I was 13 to 17 years old, I studied in a Sekolah Menegah Kebangsaan Pulau Nyior. When I was 19 to 21 years old, I studied at UiTM MERBOK, KEDAH.
